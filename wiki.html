Sample Wikipedia Article

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia, created and edited by volunteers around the world and hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation.

History

Wikipedia was launched on January 15, 2001, by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger. It has grown rapidly to become one of the largest reference websites.

Content

Wikipedia contains millions of articles in multiple languages, covering a wide range of topics.

Editing

Anyone can edit Wikipedia articles, and the community works to ensure accuracy and neutrality.
